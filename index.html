About Me


Hi, I'm Kathleen, and I finally found a photo to use! I am not a very introspective person, so I really don't like writing about myself.

I have a BA in Computer Science and Mathematics and really enjoy coding. I love the feeling of getting my code to work correctly. Sometimes I feel like jumping up and down and shouting "I did it, I did it". Sometimes getting code to work is very frustrating. I have threatened to throw my computer out the window and find some low tech job, like baking bread. But, once I overcome the obstacles, there is no turning back.

I have a stay at home mom and a substitute teacher, but I want to go back to coding, so I signed up for the class at Rutgers Coding Bootcamp to get back into the coding workforce.
